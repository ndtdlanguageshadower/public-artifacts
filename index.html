NDTD Language Shadower

Language shadowing player and practice tool for English learning.

Purpose of the app

NDTD Language Shadower helps you practice English by listening to audio and video and shadowing (repeating) what you hear. The app is designed for personal language learning: you bring your own content or use built-in topics, and the app lets you play media with subtitles, save bookmarks, and record your voice for practice.

Features

  • Import media from your device, Google Drive, OneDrive, or paste URLs (e.g. Dropbox, direct links) to stream or download for offline playback.
  • Scan book pages with the camera to create audio with subtitles for shadowing.
  • Auto-generated audio from topics (e.g. job interview, daily routines) for listening practice.
  • Playback with subtitles, bookmarks, and speed control.
  • Voice recording to practice shadowing; recordings stay on your device.

How we use Google user data

If you choose to connect Google Drive, we request read-only access to your Google Drive (the “See and download all your Google Drive files” permission). We use this only so that you can:

  • Browse your own Drive folders and files inside the app.
  • Select specific files (e.g. audio or video) you want to import for offline listening and shadowing.
  • Download only those files you explicitly choose into the app.

We do not read, upload, edit, or delete any file without you selecting it. We do not access your Gmail, contacts, or any other Google data. Your Google sign-in tokens are stored securely on your device (Keychain) and are not stored on our servers. For more detail, see our Privacy Policy.
