Getting started

  • Import media (audio/video + subtitles) and practice shadowing with playback controls.
  • Scan a page to create an audio track with subtitles (on-device OCR + speech synthesis).
  • Auto Generate to download practice audio from a topic (availability may depend on free-use limits and optional purchases).

Importing media

You can import from:

  • Files on your device (Files app, local storage)
  • Google Drive / OneDrive (sign in to browse and download files you choose)
  • URLs (examples: Dropbox share links, direct file links)

After importing, the app stores the file locally so you can play it offline. Subtitles are matched by name: if you import MyVideo.mp4, the app will look for MyVideo.srt.

Subtitles (SRT)

  • Preferred format: SRT (.srt)
  • If subtitles are not showing, confirm the SRT file is valid and timecodes look like 00:00:01,000.
  • If you imported a subtitle but it doesn’t match, try renaming it to match the media filename.

Playback tips

  • Speed control: slow down to improve accuracy, then increase gradually.
  • Bookmarks: save difficult sections and repeat them daily.
  • Background audio: the app supports background playback for listening practice.

Recording your voice (Microphone)

If the app asks for microphone access, it’s needed only to record your shadowing practice. Your recordings are saved on your device.

If recording fails, check:

  • iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone → enable NDTD Language Shadower
  • Bluetooth headset permissions / connection
  • Silent mode / volume settings

Karaoke Shadow (record mode)

Karaoke Shadow can optionally record a session (screen + microphone) so you can share your practice. When recording is enabled, iOS may show a system recording indicator.

The recorded file is saved inside the app and can be shared using the iOS share sheet.

Scan (Camera)

Scan uses the camera to capture a page and extracts text on-device. The captured image is processed on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

If camera access is denied, enable it in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera.

Auto Generate

Auto Generate downloads practice audio based on a topic you choose. The app contacts our backend to generate and deliver the audio. To reduce abuse, Auto Generate may enforce free-use limits and may require a one-time purchase for additional generations.

If Auto Generate is not working, check:

  • Network connection (try disabling VPN)
  • If you recently installed the app on a new device, try again after a few minutes
  • If you purchased but it still shows locked, wait for the App Store purchase to complete and try again

In-app purchases (tips / unlocks)

Purchases are handled by Apple. If the purchase sheet does not appear or takes too long:

  • Check Screen Time restrictions (In-app Purchases)
  • Check network/VPN
  • Try again later
