NDTD Language Shadower

v3.0 · Language shadowing player and practice tool for English learning.

Purpose of the app

NDTD Language Shadower helps you practice English by listening to audio and video and shadowing (repeating) what you hear. The app is designed for personal language learning: you bring your own content or use built-in topics, and the app lets you play media with subtitles, save bookmarks, and record your voice for practice.

Features

  • Import media from your device, Google Drive, OneDrive, or paste URLs (e.g. Dropbox, direct links) to stream or download for offline playback.
  • Scan book pages with the camera to create audio with subtitles for shadowing (on-device OCR + speech synthesis).
  • Auto-generated audio from predefined topics for listening practice (may require an in-app purchase after free usage is consumed).
  • Playback with subtitles, bookmarks, and speed control.
  • Voice recording to practice shadowing; recordings stay on your device.
  • Karaoke Shadow (optional) with screen + microphone recording for sharing (may require an in-app purchase for recording mode).
  • Optional tips to support development.

How we use Google and Microsoft user data

If you choose to connect Google Drive or OneDrive, the app requests access only so you can browse and import the specific files you select for language learning. OAuth tokens are stored securely on your device (Keychain) and are not stored on our servers.

  • Google Drive: We request read-only Drive scope. We use it only to list your Drive files/folders and download files you choose.
  • OneDrive: We request Microsoft Graph scopes needed to list and download your files you choose (and to refresh access). We do not upload, edit, or delete your files.

We do not access Gmail, contacts, calendars, or other unrelated Google/Microsoft data. For more detail, see our Privacy Policy.
